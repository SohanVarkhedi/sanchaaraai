Dataset

298,450 raw records filtered to 115,400 approved. Period: Nov 2023 – Mar 2024. Columns: lat/lon, timestamp, violation type, vehicle type, police station.

Clustering

DBSCAN eps=0.003, min_samples=50. Downtown giant cluster sub-clustered at eps=0.0015 into 20 zones. Final: 120 hotspots, 4.9% noise discarded.

Impact Score

0.5 × count_norm + 0.3 × rush_frac + 0.2 × severity_norm

Severity weights: HGV=1.0, Scooter=0.2 (assumptions). Rush-hour = IST 07–12.

Shift Deployment

Officer needs per shift (morning 06–12, afternoon 12–18, night 18–06) using real violation counts ÷ shift hours over dataset span. Throughput: 4 vph (assumption).

Tow Truck

Assigned only where heavy vehicles (HGV/Bus/Lorry/Tanker) exceed 20% of violations in a hotspot.
